How It Works

From order to memorial in just a few steps.

01

Place Your Order

Choose your stone style and size, then tell us about the person you want to honour — their name, dates, and a short personal message.

02

Build Their Page

We set up a private tribute page where you can upload photos, share stories, and add memories. You can update it at any time.

03

Receive Your Stone

Your handcrafted stone arrives engraved with a unique QR code. Place it wherever feels right — a garden, a shelf, a graveside.
