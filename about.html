✦ Our Story

Built by Builders,
For the World

We're a lean, passionate team from Dublin, Ireland — obsessed with crafting smart products, tech or otherwise, that make everyday life simpler and more connected.

🚀

Our Mission

To build smart products that remove friction from real-world problems. Whether it's a mobile app, a clever tool, or something entirely different — we design for real people, in real situations, with real constraints.

Who We Are

Builders Who Ship Real Things

We are a team of passionate builders committed to simplifying the complex. Our expertise spans mobile engineering, AI integration, UX design, and beyond — we make smart products, not just software.

Our flagship product, eRate, is proof that great ideas become great products. We're building a growing portfolio of smart solutions — with plenty more in the pipeline.

Our Values

What Drives Us

💡
Innovation First
We push boundaries with AI and modern tech to solve problems that matter — not just build for the sake of building.
🎨
Design Obsessed
Every pixel, every interaction is considered. Great software should feel effortless and look beautiful.

Human Centred
